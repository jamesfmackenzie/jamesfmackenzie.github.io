The Micomsoft SC-512N1-L/DVI is one of those capture cards that can produce impressively clean results if you understand its limits.

## Core Idea

Using an RGB SCART cable and careful post-processing, it is possible to get a very sharp, near pixel-perfect result from this card.

## Useful Angle for the Post

- show what scaling decisions matter in post

## Important Caveat

If I were doing this from scratch today, I would probably use an OSSC first and capture a 1080p output from that instead. It gives far more control over horizontal sampling and sync than the capture card does on its own.
