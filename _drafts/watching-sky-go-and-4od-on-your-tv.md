Streaming services often support a frustratingly narrow set of playback devices, especially when screen mirroring is involved.

## Officially Supported

- Xbox 360
- PlayStation 3
- PlayStation 4

## Known or Suspected Problems

- AirPlay mirroring blocked
- Chromecast tab casting questionable
- Miracast support inconsistent

## Still Worth Testing

- Direct HDMI cable
- Miracast from a laptop
- AirPlay from macOS
- Third-party tools like AirFlow or AirParrot

## What the Final Post Should Do

- Separate officially supported paths from hacks and workarounds
- Record what was actually tested
- Note which approaches fail because of DRM rather than bandwidth or app support
