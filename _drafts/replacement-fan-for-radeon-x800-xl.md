Old graphics cards often fail in very boring ways, and worn-out fans are high on the list. This draft is about replacing the fan on a Radeon X800 XL rather than trying to source a whole new cooler.

## Starting Point

The original note says the fix was simple in the end:

- identify the fan model
- buy a replacement from eBay
- install it

That is already enough for a useful practical post.

## Useful Details to Add
