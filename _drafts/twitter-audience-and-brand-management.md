Posting everything through one social account may feel authentic, but it does not automatically make the account useful to other people.

## Core Idea

Think of your interests as a Venn diagram with you at the centre. Very few people are interested in the exact full overlap. Many more people sit in one outer circle.

That creates a tension:

- one account feels personal and real
- multiple accounts may serve specific audiences better

## What the Final Post Should Explore

- when broad authenticity helps
- when topic focus matters more
- whether separate accounts are worth the maintenance overhead
- how to think about audience without becoming fake or overly managed
