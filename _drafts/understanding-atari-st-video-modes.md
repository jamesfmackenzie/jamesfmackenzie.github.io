The Atari ST has a small number of video modes, but they behave differently enough to confuse modern capture and display setups.

## Main Modes to Cover

- Low resolution
- Medium resolution
- High resolution

## Why This Matters

- Some modes are RGB only
- Some displays only accept specific sync and scan rates
- High-resolution monochrome behaves very differently from low and medium resolution colour modes
