The unit to have is the DOL-001. This is the only unit that has the digital AV out (for component, progressive scan and also HDMI/VGA adapters to work).

  

Some DOL-001 models also have an “serial port 2” that can be used with an SD2SP2 to load homebrew games, or with a GC homebrew network adapter to play e.g. Mario Kart double dash online.

  

Even if the unit doesn’t have the extra serial port, you can still use an “SD Gecko” memory card with the necessary SD card to play homebrew games. But the SDSP2 is nicer since it’s tucked out the way and you don’t need to switch memory cards to play ISOs


You probably also want a US unit since US game often support progressive scan mode
