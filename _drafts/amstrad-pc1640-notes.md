## Scope of the Post

The PC1640 is interesting because it sits at the intersection of:

- IBM PC compatible history
- unusual display options
- bundled GEM software
- restoration and upgrade work

Rather than start with lots of tiny articles, this draft aims to gather the major topics in one place first.

## Hardware Notes

The final version should describe:

- model variants
- floppy and hard drive configurations
- mouse and joystick support
- monochrome versus colour display behaviour

One especially useful point from the notes:

- the monochrome display defaults to a Hercules-style mode
- games will not display unless they support Hercules or another suitable monochrome mode
- the machine can also emulate EGA on a monochrome display, which is worth demonstrating visually
