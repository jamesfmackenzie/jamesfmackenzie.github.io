About Me

My name is James Mackenzie. I'm a Developer and Tech Lead at Goldman Sachs but this blog, it's content and opinions are my own. I started my career in IT infrastructure, but moved into software and spent most of the last 5 years working with Java and .NET. Today I also do a lot of web programming - especially in AngularJS.

I code in my personal time too, sharing my experiences on this blog. I've a million dot com ideas, but they never get my serious attention. I'm a serial hoarder of video games and retro computing hardware - gotta catch 'em all! I'm a Getting Things Done fanatic. I love automation and productivity hacks - scripting and command line FTW!
